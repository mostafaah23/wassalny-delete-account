<h1>Wassalny - Account Deletion Request</h1>

<p>If you would like to delete your Wassalny account and associated data, please follow the steps below:</p>

<ol>
<li>Open the Wassalny app</li>
<li>Go to Profile</li>
<li>Tap "Delete Account"</li>
</ol>

<p>Or send an email to: support@wassalny.com</p>

<h3>Data that will be deleted:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Name</li>
<li>Email address</li>
<li>Phone number</li>
<li>Saved addresses</li>
<li>Order history</li>
</ul>

<h3>Data retention:</h3>
<p>Some transaction records may be retained for up to 30 days for legal purposes.</p>
